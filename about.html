GET ACTION brings together athletes and employers who believe in second chances.

What we're made of

GET ACTION was founded in 2014 as a community based support project to help scholarship athletes of all ages get back on their feet and back into the sports environment. The pressures of becoming a professional or collegiate athelete are heavy, and the lifestyle can be stressful. Get Action project consists of a team of professionals to chat with, as well as an opportunity board of job listings to give you direction, purpose, and a second chance.
